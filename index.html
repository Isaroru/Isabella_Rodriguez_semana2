HARRY EDWARD STYLES COX

Holmes Chapel, Cheshire; February 1, 1994

Hi! I'm Harry Styles, a singer, songwriter, and actor from England. You might know me from my time in the boy band One Direction, or from my solo music career where I explore a mix of rock, pop, and folk sounds. I love fashion and often take risks with my clothing choices, blurring gender lines and pushing boundaries. When I'm not making music or acting, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, reading, and practicing yoga. Thanks for taking the time to get to know me a bit better!
